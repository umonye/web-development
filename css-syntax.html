Handling of grasscutters

handling of young and light animals

A young animal is lifted by the tail by holding at the base of the tail.Then grab it on its back with the other hand[just behind the front legs without squeezing too hard].

handling of medium sized grasscutters

A medium-sized grasscutters can also be lifted by the tail, but it is recommended to grab it on the back with the other hand so that there is not too weight on the tail, especially if the animal is agitated.
